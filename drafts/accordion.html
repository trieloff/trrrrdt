PS-F9 — service panel

Owner's questions, answered by the manual that shipped with the unit.

Why does the PS-F9 play records vertically?

Because gravity is a suggestion the PS-F9 declines. The linear-tracking arm clamps the record at its centre and reads it edge-on, the way a projector reads film. Nothing falls. Nothing was ever going to fall.

The needle will not lift. Is this a fault?

No. On this label the needle does not lift. Playback is continuous by design; the transport reports infinite duration and means it. If silence is required, remove the record. The PS-F9 will wait.

What cartridge ships with the unit?

A moving-magnet cartridge tuned warm — golden, never clinical. Tracking force is fixed at 1.8 grams. Do not adjust it. It was adjusted by someone who cared more than you will.

Can I clean the platter?

There is no platter. There is the spindle, the clamp, and the record standing upright like a small black moon. Wipe the record. Leave the moon alone.
