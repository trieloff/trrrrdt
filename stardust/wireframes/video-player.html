CH 01 · Title · Duration
Room is darker than the audio player. Wall is dim — the CRT screen is the primary light source. Glow radiates onto the wall above and the table surface below.
Table/shelf: dark wood or laminate. Surface catches screen glow as subtle reflection. Heavier/darker than audio player tables.
Left knob (CH): drag-rotate to change channel (alternative to swipe). Right knob (VOL): drag-rotate for volume.
Channel card: CRT on-screen display style — blocky, phosphor-coloured, with scan lines. Shows CH number, title, artist, duration. Fades after 3s.
Screen glow colour shifts with video content — this is the key atmospheric effect. The room breathes with the screen.
Interaction States
Snow / Empty
CRT shows TV snow (animated static). Room is dark — no screen glow. «Настройка...» on screen, flickering. Click device or swipe to tune in.
→
Playing
Video on CRT. Room lit by screen glow. Channel card visible briefly. Audio playing. Glow colour matches video.
Channel Change
Swipe OR rotate CH knob. 200ms static burst on CRT. Channel card flashes. New video loads. Glow crossfades. Table surface stays (or shifts subtly).

Audio Player vs Video Player
Audio (PS-F9)
• Vinyl spins, no screen
• Brighter rooms, varied surfaces
• Tap device to play/pause
• Swipe only for navigation
• Table: wood, metal, felt, plastic
Video (Yunost)
• CRT plays video, screen glows
• Darker rooms, TV is key light
• Knobs are interactive (CH + VOL)
• Swipe OR knob-rotate for nav
• Table: dark wood, glow reflections
