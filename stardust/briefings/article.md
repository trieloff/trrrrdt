# Intent

A flexible long-form template for any content that isn't the homepage, a player, or an artist page. Three primary use cases:

1. **Song articles** – lyrics, style prompt, production notes, the story behind the track
2. **Press coverage** – write-ups, reviews, interviews (all fictional, all deadpan)
3. **Liner notes** – concept project essays, thematic deep dives (TITAN metallurgy, Katabasis mythology)

The article page is the prose layer of trrrrdt.records – where the world-building goes long-form.

# Audience

- Readers who clicked a song title and want the full lyrics and context
- People exploring the fictional press universe
- Crate diggers reading liner notes for concept albums
- Mindset: lean-forward, reading mode

# Key Messages

1. Every piece of content deepens the world. A song article isn't just lyrics – it's a production manifest.
2. Press coverage is written in-universe. Reviews are by fictional publications. Interviews are conducted by fictional journalists.
3. The quality of the prose is the product – this is where the Babelsberg voice lives at full density.

# Calls to Action

- Primary: Play this track (if it's a song article – enters the audio player)
- Secondary: Read next article / explore the artist
- Tertiary: Share

# Tone

Full density. Long sentences are permitted here – the article page is where the comma gets to live. The deadpan is structural (the article format is serious) but the content can be absurd. Double encoding per Babelsberg: surface reads as legitimate music journalism, depth reads as committed fiction.

# Copy

## Song Article Structure

- **Header**: Song title (Space Grotesk 300, large) + Artist name (Fraunces) + Genre · BPM · Duration (eyebrow)
- **Style Prompt**: The Suno style prompt, displayed as a technical specification block – monospace, bordered, equipment-panel aesthetic
- **Lyrics**: Full lyrics with structure tags ([Verse], [Chorus], etc.) rendered as section dividers
- **Production Notes**: Optional – the story behind the track, thematic context, philosophical operation at work
- **Play CTA**: Inline – enters the audio player with this track

## Press Article Structure

- **Header**: Headline + Publication name (fictional) + Date + Author (fictional)
- **Body**: Standard long-form prose – paragraphs, pull quotes, inline images if available
- **Related**: Links to the artist page and other articles

## Liner Notes Structure

- **Header**: Project title + Artist(s) + Format (concept album, trilogy, etc.)
- **Body**: Essay-length prose – the intellectual framework, the thematic arc, the research behind the fiction
- **Tracklist**: Ordered list with links to individual song articles

# Imagery

## Song Articles
- No hero image by default. The style prompt block IS the visual hook.
- Optional: album art if it exists for a concept project

## Press Articles
- Hero image if available (generated or sourced)
- Pull quote styling for key passages

## Liner Notes
- Cassette-label motif as header decoration
- Section dividers using VU meter bars
